Privacy Policy

This page explains how Namadhu Jobs handles contact details and enquiry information submitted through namadhujobs.com.

Last Updated

May 19, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we may collect the details you provide, including:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address, if provided
  • Selected service
  • Business details or message

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to your enquiry, discuss your business requirements, provide service details, prepare quotations, and communicate through phone, email, or WhatsApp.

WhatsApp Enquiries

Our contact form opens WhatsApp with your enquiry message ready to send. The message is sent only when you choose to send it through WhatsApp. WhatsApp may process your message according to its own privacy practices.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to provide requested services, comply with legal requirements, or protect our business from misuse or fraud.

Data Retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as needed for communication, service follow-up, business records, or legal requirements.

Your Choices

You can contact us to request correction or removal of your enquiry details from our active records, subject to reasonable business or legal requirements.
